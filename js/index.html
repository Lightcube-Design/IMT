About IMT

Precision Engineering. Three Decades Strong.

Inland Machine Tools is part of a group of three closely associated companies delivering end-to-end precision engineering solutions for India's leading manufacturers — from automotive and pump industries to defence applications.

Our parent company, Continental Engineering Corporation, was established in 1992 and secured ISO 9001:2000 certification in 2002. Ball screw development began the same year, with our first successful sample manufactured in 2004. IMT was established in 2006 as a dedicated ball screw manufacturing unit in Bhosari, Pune.

Today we operate across 10,000 sq. ft. of covered manufacturing area with 40+ skilled engineers and 25+ trusted sub-vendors — proudly serving 50+ clients including TATA Motors, Bajaj Auto, Bharat Forge, SKF, L&T and the Indian defence sector.
